FAQ

Can I bring a plus-one?

Unfortunately due to limited capacity and seating arrangements, we cannot offer plus-ones.

Can I bring my children?

Our reception dinner will be an adults-only event but your children are more than welcome to come and celebrate with us at our ceremony.

Do you have a Gift Registry?

We do not have a gift registry. Instead, we would prefer cash gifts as we build our home together.

Is there a dress code or theme?

We'd love for our guests to join us in formal or cocktail attire. Gentlemen, feel free to wear a suit or dress shirt. Ladies, a dress or dressy separates would be great.

Will the events be in Korean or English?

The ceremony will mostly be in Korean and the reception will be both Korean and English. So it's probably a good idea to start brushing up on your Korean in Duolingo.

Who should we contact for further questions?

For further questions regarding the wedding please contact Betty on 0452621002 or Joseph on 0416578777.
